About the photographer

Meeshal Bakrania

I am a 17-year-old photographer with a growing desire to pursue photography as something more than a hobby. I am drawn to the quiet moments people often pass by: light on a flower, the shape of a car in the street, or an animal caught in a calm second of movement.

This exhibition is a place to share the work I have been building through patience, practice, and a real love for capturing life as it happens. New images and more variety will be added each week, giving visitors fresh pieces to view, collect, and buy.
